## Common Use Cases

### Freelancers & Consultants

TimeTiles helps you track billable hours, manage multiple clients, and generate professional reports for invoicing.

**Key Features:**

- Client-specific projects
- Hourly rate tracking
- Invoice-ready reports
- Time entry approval workflow

### Teams & Agencies

Coordinate team time tracking, monitor project progress, and analyze team productivity.

**Key Features:**

- Team dashboards
- Project collaboration
- Resource allocation
- Performance analytics

### Personal Productivity

Track personal projects, analyze time usage patterns, and improve work-life balance.

**Key Features:**

- Personal time tracking
- Habit tracking
- Goal setting
- Time analysis
