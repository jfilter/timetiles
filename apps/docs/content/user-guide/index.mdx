## What You Can Do

**Import** — Upload CSV, Excel, or ODS files, paste a URL, or set up automated imports on a schedule. TimeTiles detects your schema and geocodes addresses automatically.

**Explore** — Pan and zoom the map, scrub the timeline, filter by category or date range, and click events to see details. Every field in your data becomes a filter.

**Share** — Every view has a unique URL with filters, map position, and time range encoded. Bookmark it, share it, or embed it — recipients see exactly what you see.

**Manage** — Organize data into catalogs and datasets. Export everything as a ZIP archive. Delete your account with a grace period.
